SPIDERMAN

Spider-Man, a beloved character in Marvel Comics, is a superhero known for his acrobatic abilities and web-slinging powers. Created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko, Peter Parker, a high school student, gains these powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He uses his abilities to fight crime and protect New York City, balancing his superhero life with his personal life and struggles. Spider-Man's signature traits include his iconic red and blue suit, his "spider-sense" which alerts him to danger, and his witty banter while battling villains.

    PETER PARKER

    Peter Parker, also known as Spider-Man, is a fictional superhero created by Stan Lee and Steve Ditko. He's a high school student who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He uses these powers, along with his intelligence and homemade gadgets, to fight crime in New York City. Peter Parker is known for his relatable struggles, balancing his superhero life with his personal life, and for his strong moral compass, always striving to do what's right.
